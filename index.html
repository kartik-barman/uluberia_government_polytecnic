About

Uluberia Goverbment Polytecnic

Welcome to Uluberia Government Polytechnic - Uluberia Government Polytechnic under Government of West Bengal is going to start its hubmle journey offering Three years Diploma Course in Engineering from the academic session 2021- 22 with due approval from All India Council for Technical Education ( AICTE ) , Government of India and duly affiliated by the West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Developmemt , Government of West Bengal . The institute is located at Uluberia Sub - division of Howrah district connected by nearest railway Station of Birshibpur in Howrah - Kharagppur South - Eastern Railway and about 45 Km roadway distance from kolkata . The institute is surrounded by the big industrial hub , reputed degree level engineering , science , arts , commerce colleges , automobile and agricultural base of Uluberia Sub division of Howrah District . The college will have a full fledge faculties , modern infrastructural facilities , like Libraries , Laboratories , Workshop , Smart Class room , Big Computer Laboratory , Language Laboratory , Boys and Girls Common Room , Cafeteria .
